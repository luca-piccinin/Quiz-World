First you need to choose the language that will affect the entire app.
After that you have to enter your username which will be used to see the result.
Once you press the next button you can go to choose the topic on which the quiz will be made.
Once the topic has been chosen, the quiz will begin.
It will show one question at a time, after which you can write your own answer.
After that, just hit the send button to go to the next question.
At the conclusion of the questions you will be sent to the activity that will show the results.
